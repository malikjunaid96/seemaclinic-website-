About the clinic

A neighbourhood clinic built on a public-health approach to care.

Dr. Seema Qazi combines a clinical medical degree (MBBS) with a Master's in Public Health (MSPH), bringing a preventive, whole-community lens to everyday consultations — not just treating symptoms, but understanding the bigger picture of a patient's health.

As a Registered Medical Practitioner (RMP) based in Multi Gardens B-17, the clinic serves families across the sector with general consultations, women's health, child immunisations and ongoing management of chronic conditions.

MBBSBachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery — the core medical degree.
MSPHMaster of Science in Public Health — preventive & community health expertise.
RMPRegistered Medical Practitioner — licensed to practise medicine.
